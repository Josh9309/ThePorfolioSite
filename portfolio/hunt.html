Rathalos’s Hunt

Summary

Rathalos’s Hunt in I created all the art assets and the opening animatic for the game. The Game was made by my professor and we were asked to created a story and 2D art assets for the game. The game is a shoot em up where you fight on coming enemies. The story behind my game was that You are Rathlos, an experienced monster hunter working to become a master hunter. To do this Rathlos must journey to an ancient castle that has become overridden with various types of monsters and completely clear the castle in order to earn his title as Master Monster Hunter.

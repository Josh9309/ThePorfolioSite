Robot Zoo

Summary

The Robot Zoo project is a world in which Robots are exploring a zoo full of different animals and attractions. I created the world using assets from the Unity store and models I created. Through out the world I focused on implementing different game behavior algorithims such as seeking, Wandering, fleeing and more.

About Me Skills

Hello people, my name is Josh Malmquist from Topsfield, Massachusetts. I am currently a student studying game design and development at Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT). You may be wondering what makes me, well me? The answer is a couple of things, the biggest one being my passion and love for video games. I am a game programmer and one of the big reason I am a game programmer is because of the effect video games have had on my life. Video games have gotten me through some of the worst moments in my life. They have brought a smile to my face when I did not think it was possible. I want to make games that help to bring that same feeling and effect to others, which is why I am what I am. In terms others my interests I am a active member of both STAR (RIT's Sci-Fi Club) and Archery club on campus. I love animals specifically dogs, wolves, pandas, koalas, and sloths. Some of my favorite games series are: Ratchet and Clank, Jax and Daxter, Uncharted, and The Witcher Series.

Coconut Beach Jump

Summary

Coconut Beach Jump was a prototype game that I made while I was interning at Funkitron Inc. for proof of concept. The game was made in Corona SDK and was programed in Lua. Coconut Beach Jump was one of the first games I ever made. I had very little programing knowledge and did not know Lua at all before this game. This game was programed in about 2 months, and I learned a great deal from it.

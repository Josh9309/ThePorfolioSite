The Asylum

Summary

The Asylum is a 3D Modeling Project that my friends and I did in unity. The Asylum is an inside out house that we made with inspiration from a scene in hitchhiker’s guide to the galaxy. The scene is composed of a unity terrain with all the props in the scene being created by people on our team.
